In-App Reporting

How to Report Child Safety Concerns:

  1. Open the Me+U app
  2. Go to the user's profile or chat
  3. Tap the "Report" or "..." menu
  4. Select "Child Safety Concern"
  5. Provide detailed information about the concern
  6. Submit the report

Response Protocol

  • Immediate Action: Reports are reviewed within 2 hours
  • Account Suspension: Immediate suspension of reported accounts pending investigation
  • Evidence Preservation: All relevant data is preserved for law enforcement
  • Law Enforcement Cooperation: Full cooperation with authorities
